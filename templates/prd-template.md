# PRD Template

Use this template for all product requirements documents.

---

## Pre-Writing Checklist

- [ ] Have I verified this problem exists in the wild?
- [ ] Have I talked to users about this?
- [ ] What are competitors doing?
- [ ] What does the customer actually experience?

---

## PRD Structure

### Problem (MUST BE FIRST)
**What problem are we solving?**
> [Clear problem statement]

**Who has this problem?**
> [Target users]

**Why does this matter?**
> [Impact and importance]

**What's the current state?**
> [Current experience and pain points]

---

### Solution
**How do we solve this problem?**
> [High-level approach]

**What are the key features?**
> [Key features and capabilities]

**What's the user experience?**
> [How users will experience this]

---

### Success Metrics

**Controllable Metrics:**
> [Metrics we can directly influence]
> - Metric 1: [Target]
> - Metric 2: [Target]

**Track-Only Metrics:**
> [Metrics we'll track but not control]
> - Metric 1: [For context]
> - Metric 2: [For context]

**Guardrails:**
> [Metrics we'll monitor to ensure we don't break anything]
> - Guardrail 1: [Threshold]
> - Guardrail 2: [Threshold]

---

## Post-Writing Checklist

- [ ] Does this have a clear PROBLEM paragraph?
- [ ] Does the solution solve the problem?
- [ ] Are metrics controllable vs track-only?
- [ ] What are the guardrails?
- [ ] Have I verified this problem exists?

---

**Remember:** Problem paragraph first, then solution!
