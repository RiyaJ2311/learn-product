# Ownership & Branding Audit

Audit personal branding, ownership clarity, and professional identity.

## Focus Areas

1. **Brand Consistency**
   - Name presentation, title/role clarity
   - Professional positioning
   - Voice and tone consistency

2. **Attribution & Ownership**
   - Work attribution clarity
   - Role in projects clearly stated
   - Team vs solo work distinction

3. **Professional Identity**
   - Unique value proposition
   - Expertise areas highlighted
   - Personal philosophy/approach

4. **Contact & Social Proof**
   - Email, social links visibility
   - Testimonials attribution
   - Professional networks

5. **Credibility Signals**
   - Company names, metrics
   - Years of experience
   - Portfolio permissions

6. **Copyright & Legal**
   - Copyright notices
   - Portfolio rights statements

## Deliverable

Report with:
- Brand Strength Score (0-100)
- Ownership Clarity per page
- Missing Elements list
- Inconsistencies found
- Recommendations for strengthening
