## Key Framework: Ian McAllister's Communication Principles

From the Lenny's Podcast episode with Ian McAllister (ex-Amazon, Uber, Airbnb):

### 1. Answer First, Then Explain
> "Don't bury the lead. Start with the answer, then provide context."

### 2. Grade Yourself After Every Communication
> "After every important communication, ask: Did it land? What would I do differently?"

### 3. Trust is the Currency
> "Every unclear communication costs you trust. Every clear one builds it."

- Track your scores in weekly reflections
