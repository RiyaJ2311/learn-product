# Learning Paths Overview

Choose the path that fits your timeline and goals.

---

## Path 1: Quick Wins (2 weeks)
**Best for:** Immediate behavior changes, urgent feedback themes

### Week 1: Communication Clarity
- **Focus:** Answer First + Impact Translation
- **Time:** 1.5 hours reading + daily practice
- **Goal:** Every message lands on first read

### Week 2: Ownership & Execution
- **Focus:** Permissionless Execution + Metric Ownership
- **Time:** 1.5 hours reading + daily practice
- **Goal:** Make decisions without unnecessary escalation

**Outcome:** Immediate improvement in communication and decision-making speed.

---

## Path 2: Deep Dive (6 weeks)
**Best for:** Comprehensive skill building, career development

### Weeks 1-2: Communication Clarity
- **Week 1:** Answer First (30 mins reading + practice)
- **Week 2:** Impact Translation + Self-Grading (1.5 hours reading + practice)
- **Goal:** Master all communication skills

### Weeks 3-4: Data Storytelling
- **Week 3:** Data Triangulation + Baseline First (1.5 hours reading + practice)
- **Week 4:** Cohort Analysis (45 mins reading + practice)
- **Goal:** Make data-driven decisions confidently

### Weeks 5-6: Product Strategy + Leadership
- **Week 5:** Working Backwards + Outside-In Thinking (2 hours reading + practice)
- **Week 6:** PRD Quality + Earn Trust (1.5 hours reading + practice)
- **Goal:** Think strategically and build trust

**Outcome:** Comprehensive PM skill development across all areas.

---

## Path 3: Feedback-Driven (Ongoing)
**Best for:** Personalized improvement based on your specific gaps

### Step 1: Map Your Feedback
- Complete `01-Your-Feedback-Map.md`
- Identify your top 2-3 feedback themes
- Map themes to modules

### Step 2: Focus on Top Themes
- Start with your highest-priority theme
- Complete the relevant module (1-2 weeks per module)
- Practice daily, self-grade weekly

### Step 3: Iterate
- After each module, update your feedback map
- Remove themes you've mastered
- Add new themes as they emerge

**Outcome:** Targeted improvement on your specific gaps.

---

## Path 4: Module-by-Module (Self-Paced)
**Best for:** Learning at your own pace, deep mastery

### Recommended Order:
1. **Communication Clarity** (Foundation - do this first)
2. **Data Storytelling** (Apply communication skills to data)
3. **Ownership & Execution** (Build trust through execution)
4. **Product Strategy** (Think strategically)
5. **Leadership & Trust** (Build influence)

### For Each Module:
- Read the module index (5 mins)
- Complete each part (30-45 mins reading)
- Practice for 1-2 weeks
- Self-grade and reflect weekly
- Move to next module when ready

**Outcome:** Deep mastery of each skill area.

---

## Choosing Your Path

### Choose Quick Wins If:
- You have urgent feedback themes
- You need immediate improvement
- You have limited time (2 weeks)

### Choose Deep Dive If:
- You want comprehensive development
- You have 6 weeks to invest
- You're preparing for a promotion or new role

### Choose Feedback-Driven If:
- You have specific feedback themes
- You want personalized improvement
- You prefer targeted learning

### Choose Module-by-Module If:
- You want to learn at your own pace
- You prefer deep mastery over speed
- You have ongoing development time
